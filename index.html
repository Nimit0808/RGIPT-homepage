Welcome to RGIPT

Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Petroleum Technology, established in 2008, is India's premier institution dedicated to petroleum and energy education, recognised as an Institution of National Importance by the Government of India.

World-Class Campus

State-of-the-art infrastructure spread across a lush green campus in Amethi.

Cutting-Edge Research

Pioneering research in petroleum, energy, and sustainable technologies.

Energy of the Future

Leading India's transition towards renewable and clean energy solutions.

Vibrant Student Life

A rich campus culture with sports, clubs, fests, and student activities.

Greetings to all! It is with great pride that I welcome you to Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Petroleum Technology. RGIPT stands as a beacon of excellence in petroleum and energy education, committed to nurturing the next generation of leaders who will power India's energy future.
